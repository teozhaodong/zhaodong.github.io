FINAL ASSEMBLY

BUILD PROCESS

Throughout the building process, I encountered issues with hiding the wires and storing of the circuits and power supply. Hence, I decided the add another layer of wood to hide the wires between them. Also, I 3D printed a box to keep the circuits and power supply.

CIRCUIT ASSEMBLY

The circuit comprises of two servo motors, two LED strps, Ultrasonic Sensor and Buzzer connected to the breadboard and Arduino Uno. Once the Ultrasonic Sensor senses movement from the trash, the servo motors will turn 90 degrees out and the buzzer will play the mario theme song. The two LED strips will constantly be lit up to attract the general public to gain their attention towards the bin.

ARDUINO CODES

The codes used in Arduino firstly consists of the frequency of each music note used for the Mario theme song. Followed by the sequence of the RGB LED lights. Finally, the codes for the Ultrasonic Sensor and Servo Motors.
